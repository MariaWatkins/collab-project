Story

Joe Bloggs is an average teenager starting a new school. Going into the school with a positive attitude, completely unaware that he was entering a world of social media. First person he meets is Facebook, a very controlling and enthusiastic character. As Joe accepts his friend request he gets introduced to others such as Whatsapp, iMessage, Kik, Twitter, Instagram, Snapchat and Flappy Bird. Joe's frustration builds up from being followed and harassed by his new friend Facebook and eventually their friendship crumbles.
